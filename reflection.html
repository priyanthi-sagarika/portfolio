My Reflection

I felt proud and satisfied with how the students connected deeply with the lesson. Their emotional and intellectual engagement was rewarding. However, I was also concerned about ensuring that all students had equal opportunities to participate, as some required more guidance during group activities. This feeling highlighted the challenge of balancing the needs of all learners within the limited time frame.

Overall, the experience had many positive aspects. The use of technology and the thought-provoking story successfully engaged the students and improved their four skills. The class was well-managed, and the objectives were achieved within the time frame. However, some students' varying levels of ability presented a challenge, as not all were equally prepared or confident in group activities. This difference in readiness slightly impacted the lesson's flow for certain students.

Reflecting on the situation, I realized that while the lesson structure and content were effective, more attention could have been given to differentiated instruction. Some students needed more tailored support to fully engage with the tasks, and providing additional scaffolding would have ensured a more inclusive learning experience.

If I were to conduct this lesson again, I would focus on pre-lesson preparation. Providing students with preparatory materials or instructions beforehand could help bridge the gap in readiness. I would also integrate differentiated activities to cater to individual learning needs, ensuring that all students can participate meaningfully.

In the future, I would also advocate for greater flexibility in resources. Given the restrictions in Sri Lankan schools, allowing students to bring their devices with proper permissions could enhance lesson plans and make activities more accessible. This approach would not only improve engagement but also help develop students' ICT skills alongside their competence. As a teacher who tries to bring variety to the classroom, I am not demotivated by the challenges I faced but will try my best to achieve my objectives in the teaching and learning process.
